About SMIT

Set near the banks of the perennial river Teesta, SMIT has a campus like none other.
Established in the year 1997, SMIT is a constituent college of Sikkim Manipal University located in Tadong, Gangtok.
It is a part of the celebrated Manipal group that has made its mark in the field of education and health services in India and abroad.
The institution has assent from the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE) and UGC (University Grants Commission).

Research

Highly motivated research programs that help the community at large.

The motive of science is to make life better for the billions who live and breathe the same air as us. Therefore, research is a necessary tool in making that happen and is the lifeblood of a sound academic institute. SMIT has a well-functioning research environment with students and faculty both taking an active part in it. Several scholars have been awarded a Ph.D. and many have been registered with a doctorate. Organizations such as DST, DBT, DRDO, and AICTE approve and finance research projects sponsored for the faculty.

Placements

Getting a job and making one, are two branches of employment, and SMIT does not wane in any of them. It holds a great placement record with the students being in well-known national and international organizations such as Microsoft, FL Smidth, TCS, Infosys, HCL, Tech Mahindra, Wipro, Accenture, Satyam, Microland and many more. The institution has grown over the years and is well on its tracks to achieving greater heights in the coming future.

Start-Ups

Entrepreneurship is a very creative, towering, and management-oriented vocation and needs proper supervision. SMIT has always backed minds that cartel to nurture their dreams and hence, along with the NITI Aayog and the Government of India, SMIT has envisioned the first Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) in North East India. A state-of-the-art facility, the AIC has been the perfect hood for young businesspersons with objectives and skills that can be nurtured .
