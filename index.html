About IAESTE CI SMU

Sikkim Manipal University(SMU) became a Cooperating Institute of IAESTE on Tuesday, April 5, 2016.
A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) was signed between IAESTE India and SMU to initiate the much-awaited exchange of students with various universities and organisations abroad.

Origin

2016

IAESTE CISMU started with their operations from August of the same year, in the campus of the Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology. Amongst the faculty members, Mr P. Barthakur was chosen as the lead and to take care of the committee. With new engagements and commitments, our former faculty Coordinator, Dr. Santonu Savapondit, has now taken over as the Head of the International Collaboration Cell at SMIT and with this, Mr. Soham Das leads as the current faculty coordinator of IAESTE CISMU.

Since the foundation of IAESTE CISMU, a total of eight(8) worldwide internship opportunities have been put up by the committee and has also witnessed two(2) incoming exchange students as well as two (2) students going abroad for their industrial experiences. IAESTE CISMU has played a vital role in providing undergraduate students with hands-on practical work experience and an effective global perspective.

VISION AND MISSION

IAESTE CISMU is committed to being an integral part of the academic experience for one and all at Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology by providing them with academically sound, credit-bearing education programs abroad that will help develop intercultural competence and nurture globally-relevant leadership skills. In cooperation with academic and service units, IAESTE CI SMU provides comprehensive support for the development, implementation and coordination of safe and accessible programs abroad.

Mentorship Initiatives:

CV Enhancement Programme to guide students with a direction for future prospects in their respective fields. This involved mentoring the freshersabout utilising the semester breaks to prepare for better opportunities at internships.

Workshops conducted by respective departments on technological arenas such as Artificial Intelligence, Robotics so that students receive knowledge apart from curriculum

Privilege of working on certified projects under faculty guidance.

SMIT

Set near the banks of the perennial river Teesta, SMIT has a campus like none other. Established in the year 1997, SMIT is a constituent college of Sikkim Manipal University located in Tadong, Gangtok. It is a part of the celebrated Manipal group that has made its mark in the field of education and health services in India and abroad. The institution has assent from the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE) and UGC (University Grants Commission).
